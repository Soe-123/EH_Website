Engaging people living with dementia through the creative arts

Process Workshops

Information

What
A deep-dive into a particular creative process, for a suggested number of 4-weeks, but can be more or less depending on your needs.
Who
For a group of carers or community groups with an existing cohort of members.
When
We'll find a time that suits you.
How
These are normally in-person, however in-light of the pandemic we are happy to devise a proposal that can work online.
Funding
These are paid for in advance following an quote and invoice. Depending on your expectations, price will be a combination of an hourly facilitation rate, a small amount of preperation time and cost of materials.
