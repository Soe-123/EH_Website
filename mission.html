Engaging people living with dementia through the creative arts

Mission

To encourage people living with dementia to engage with the creative arts. To conduct collaborative and contemporary art workshops in care homes, nursing homes, community settings, art spaces and day centres. With each workshop a practicing artist will work co-creatively or one to one with people experiencing dementia. These creative engagements will also be relevant to many isolated people living with affects of Dementia, but have not been diagnosed.

Our vision is that participants engaging in our workshops will experience these long term outcomes:

  • Better quality of life
  • Better cognitive ability and in turn an understanding of how to stimulate themselves
  • Better physical ability, causing themselves less harm
  • Less of an expense to the NHS
  • More confortable with social interaction

Day to day our goal is that participants are:

  • Calmer in oneself and less agitated
  • Reminiscent of their short term creativity
  • Less challenging and stressful for their carers
  • More competent at carrying out day to day operations
  • More of an ability to build a present picture of who they are
